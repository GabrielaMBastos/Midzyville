Who is ITZY?

ITZY is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group consists of five members: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted on February 12, 2019, with the release of their single album It'z Different.

The name ITZY is a reference to the Korean phrase "있지", which translates to "there is". It can also mean "have it" or "got it". Basically, ITZY "has it" or they "got it", whatever fans want or need.

Some of ITZY titles given by the industry, media and professionals, are, Monster Rookies, Global Kpop Representative Performers, One of Kpop Top Girl Groups, 4th Generation Leaders and Kpop's 4th Gen Queens.

FANDOM NAME: MIDZY. The name comes from the Korean word 믿어, which means trust, "Means that the fans trust ITZY and ITZY trusts the fans in return."
